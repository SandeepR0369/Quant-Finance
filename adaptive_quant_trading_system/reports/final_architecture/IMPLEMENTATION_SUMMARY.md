## Key architecture decisions
- Centralize data normalization in loader to avoid downstream KeyError and inconsistent schemas.
- Use next‑bar execution to prevent look-ahead and keep daily experiments reproducible.
- Stops execute before rebalancing (defensive policy).
- Keep optimizer lightweight to avoid overfitting risk.

## Realism improvements
- Volatility-based sizing
- Stop enforcement and stop event logging
- Conservative intrabar assumptions for stop fills
